    Three N.F.L. Quarterbacks. Three Allens. Which Is Which?

    By VICTOR MATHER NOV. 6, 2019

    Can you tell Brandon, Josh and Kyle Allen apart?

    If you are a football fan, you know about the Allens, the three starting N.F.L. quarterbacks who share a surname, but are unrelated.

    You know that Josh Allen is the second-year man who has led the Buffalo Bills to a 6-2 record, Kyle Allen is the Carolina Panther who has been subbing for Cam Newton, and Brandon Allen is the Denver Bronco who got his first shot this week and filled in admirably for Joe Flacco.

    But is that all you know? Let’s see if you can keep all these Allens straight in this quiz.
